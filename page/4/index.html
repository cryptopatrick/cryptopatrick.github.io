ToyModel: Skynex AntiDrone Weapon System ::Draft

September 26, 2024 Mathematics Modelling Programming

I read a conversation on Twitter regarding a new weapon system from german company Reihmetal. The system is called Skynex. Just out of curiousity, but also to practice using the math I’m learning to model stuff to understand how things might fit together. Model Components and Variables 1.Search Radar Detection (10 km range) Drones are detected at a distance of up to $R_\text{search} = 10km$. The system can track and prioritize multiple drones simultaneously. Engagement Range (4 km) The gun system engages drones when they come within $R_\text{engage} = 4km$. Number of Cannons in a Battery For $50$ drones, at least $N_\text{cannons} = 7$. Gun Performance The cannon can intercept up to $N_\text{drones} = 8$ small drones within a few seconds. Each cannon has a capacity of $C_\text{rounds} = 250$ rounds The system uses proximity ammo, reducing the need for direct hits. Let’s assume $N_\text{rounds} = 5$ rounds are used per drone on average. Drone Characteristics Drones travel at speed $v_\text{drone}$ in $m/s$ which will vary depending on the type of drone. There could be up to $50$ drones approaching, with a spacing of $d_\text{drone}$ between them (either as a swarm or spread out). Radar and Sensor Performance The system employs radar, TV-tracking, IR-tracking, and a laser range finder, making jamming or evasion by drones extremely unlikely. Let’s assume the radar can handle simultaneous tracking of $N_\text{radar} = 100$ drones. Engagement Timing Engagement is automatic, based on prioritization by speed and course. Reaction time $t_\text{reaction}$ between detection and engagement is determined by sensor processing and mechanical limitations of the gun.
